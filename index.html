About Darwen Live

Darwen Live is a free-to-attend annual music festival held in the picturesque Lancashire mill-town of Darwen, on the West Pennine Moors.

The event is organised and run by volunteers and funded by Darwen Town Council, Blackburn with Darwen Borough Council, and private sponsors.

Darwen Live is a community event and we thrive on your input and ideas. Anybody who would like to lend some time or suggestions to support the event can contact us below.

We can't wait to be with you all again and to enjoy some fabulous music in our brilliant town.

Darren, Paul, Benn, Lewis all the Darwen Live contributors.
